## How this started

It started with a route. The first time we crossed the cordillera from Argentina into Chile by a pass almost nobody uses, we knew that was the trip we wanted to share. Not the brochure version. The other one — the one you learn on the road.

## How we run trips

- Small groups: maximum eight bikes per expedition.
- Every route is ridden at least twice before we sell it.
- Own support: chase vehicle, spares, comms gear.
- Lodging where lodging exists: hostería, mountain refuge, town posada.

## What you won't find here

Catalogue trips. Comfort as the pitch. Hollow promises. What you will find are routes measured in kilometers, days, and altitude — and a team who has already ridden them.
